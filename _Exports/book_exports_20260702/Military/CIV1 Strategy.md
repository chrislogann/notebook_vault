CIV1 Strategy
- Expand, build, and develop
- What are my strategic point?
- Does my industrial output meet or exceed strategic and environmental realities?
- What role does each city play? Military, industrial, economic or political?
- Does my government meet my output expectations?
- What is my military focus? Naval, army, air, hybrid doctrines?
- Will I achieve victory militarily or scientifically?
- Is my civilization a major or minor power?

General strategy:
- Defend cities with garrisons and planes
- Patrol between cities with mobile units (cavalry, chariot, or armor) 
- Pave path to victory space race or military
- Understand the balance of power between civilizations
- Evaluate economic dynamics. Free-trade or closed?
- Analyze key environmental areas: Mountains, forests, maritime straits

Hybrid war tactics:
- Weaponize pollution mechanic to cause global warming? 
- Scorched earth tactics?
